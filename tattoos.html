Permanent Expressions

This work was made whilst also working on a series that considers portraiture and explores both what a portrait is and how individuals use facial expressions to convey themselves. This series furthers the exploration of self-expression and personality by looking at a very different, and very permanent way, that people choose to express themselves.

Historically, tattoos have been used in religion, as punishment, to show status or accomplishments, and have served many other purposes. Today, tattoos have become an art form, and while many would still consider them controversial, to the millennial generation especially, they have become a form of self-expression.
