Expressions

Focused on youth still in or just out of their undergraduate years, this series explores how different people express their emotions, react to similar questions or comments, talk about their lives, and react to a camera. While everyone is at a similar point in their lives, they each experience it differently, and personality plays a large role in how people live. Whilst trying to capture the unique personality of each person in a single photograph, it became harder and harder to define who these people were. Instead, it is through many images, a collection of moments and conversations, that these people are best understood. It is instead the search, the frustration, the laughter, and the understanding that these people are made up of so much more than one simple image can show.
